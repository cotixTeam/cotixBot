Register for the Minor League Esports Season 11

MLE is a fun, friendly gaming community focused on organized competitive leagues for Rocket League players of all skill levels. Our goal is to create a "professional experience" for our players, with weekly streams on Twitch, 32 unique franchises, and a season format based on pro sports and esports leagues.

Season 11 registration is open

Apply at mlesports.gg/join to join..

More info

Currently, MLE has 5 leagues. You are placed in a league based on your highest peak MMR in competitive 2s or 3s.

When you join the league, you are assigned a salary. Salary is the metric by which League Operations balances teams. Every player is assigned a salary, which is calculated by taking a player’s peak MMR from Doubles or Standard, rounding down to the nearest 50, then dividing it by 100.

The current league breakdowns by salary are as follows:

  • Foundation League: 10.0 and below (1000MMR and Below)
  • Academy League: 10.5 - 12.5 (1050MMR - 1250MMR)
  • Champion League: 13.0 - 14.5 (1300MMR - 1450MMR)
  • Master League: 15.0 - 16.5 (1500MMR - 1650MMR)
  • Premier League: 17.0+ (1700MMR and Above)
